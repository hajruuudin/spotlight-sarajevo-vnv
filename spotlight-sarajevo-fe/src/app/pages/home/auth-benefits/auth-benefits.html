Create an account for more!

SpotlightSarajevo can be used without an account, but to have the best experience, make sure to create an account! Creating an account unlocks a multitude of new features for You, including:
  • Keeping track of items in collections
  • Creating custom collections
  • Leaving and viewing reviews for spots and organisers
  • Create community requests to help admins add new events and spots
  • Tracking which spots you've visited and events you've attended!
  • Get recomendations based on your favourite categories!
